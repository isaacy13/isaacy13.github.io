Expressing My Interests

Spoiler: I enjoy developing

Senior year of high school, I was unsure of the future. Although I always knew college was the next step, I had no idea what major I wanted nor what I'd do for a living.

Luckily, I had stumbled across a program known as CS50, and so for the pandemic-ridden summer of my senior year, I was doing introductory C++ exercises. Suprisingly, I enjoyed it a lot; somehow, manipulating the computer to do what I wanted was really appealing (may have to do with me playing video games from a young age?). From then, I knew computer science was the major meant for me.

Fast forward a year later, after getting clobbered with harder yet much more interesting classes, I have never looked back on my career path. So far, I'm most interested in:

  • Full-stack web, desktop, and mobile development
  • Applying machine learning to real-world scenarios
  • Learning more about other areas of development

Definitely looking forward to what the future holds!
